Here's our story

We started out at our home kitchen, whipping up finger-foods for our friends wedding's and niece's birthdays. Everyone loved our food and our innovative combinations of flavors. So, what started out as a family operated business quickly grew into a fully-fledged small business. The team has also grown to from 2 to 15 people serving almost thousands of people per day

Momomia's menu includes crisp, tasty, custom-made Momos and other Chinese Food. Our food is solid and yummy. We offer a broad vegetarian and Non vegetarian menu and have sans gluten choices upon demand. Also check out the wide variety of beverages we offer to complete your meal.
